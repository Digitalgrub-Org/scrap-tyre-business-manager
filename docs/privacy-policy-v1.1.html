Privacy Policy

Scrap Tyre Business Manager · by Digitalgrub · Last updated: 2 July 2026

Scrap Tyre Business Manager ("the app") helps you record scrap tyre purchases, sales, stock, and expenses. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Accounts and sign in

You can use the app in three ways:

  • Email and password - you create an account with your email.
  • Google - you sign in with your Google account.
  • Guest - you use the app without giving an email. A guest account has no recoverable credential, so guest data cannot be restored after the app is removed or on another device unless you later add an email or Google account to it.

What we collect and store

  • Account information: your email address (for email or Google sign in) and an account identifier. Guest accounts have only an anonymous identifier, no email.
  • Business records you enter: purchases, sales, expenses, stock, and the supplier and customer names, phone numbers, and notes you choose to add.

The app does not collect location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics, and it contains no advertising.

Where your data is stored

Your account and business records are stored in the cloud using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication), with data hosted in the Mumbai, India region. Data is associated with your account so that only you can access it, and it can sync across your signed in devices. The app also keeps a local copy on your device so it works offline.

How we use your data

Your data is used only to provide the app's features to you: storing and showing your records, computing stock and profit, and syncing across your devices. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or track you across other apps or websites.

Third parties

We use Google Firebase as our infrastructure provider to authenticate accounts and store data on our behalf. Google processes this data under its own terms. We do not share your business records with any other third party.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account exists. To delete your data, clear demo entries from Settings, delete individual records in the app, or contact us to request deletion of your account and its data. Removing the app from a device does not by itself delete cloud data for email or Google accounts.

Security

Access is restricted so that each account can only read and write its own data. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections.

Children

The app is intended for business use and is not directed at children.

Changes

We may update this policy and will change the date above when we do.
